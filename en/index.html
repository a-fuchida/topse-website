TOP Software Enginner

Education Program For Top Software Engineers

Cultivating the “Super Architects” supporting information-based society.

The Top SE Project is a practical education program aiming to cultivate software engineers who have acquired highly advanced development techniques based on the concept, “intellectual manufacturing education based on science.”

Achievement of enrolling in Top SE

The Top SE Project provides the following three benefits by providing educational programs fusing practice in the industrial world and theory in the academic world.

  1. To learn state-of-the-art software technologies
  2. To learn technologies applicable to actual development occasions
  3. To improve problem-solving abilities

Who should attend to Top SE

Top SE proposes beneficial curriculum for who want to improve their engineering quality as like.

  1. Earning the up-to-date knowledge, and positively overcoming the limitation of current result.
  2. Soundly studying the knowledge of software engineering from its fundamental.
  3. Discovering new horizons with newly earned knowledge, and expanding their result of work.

Result in completing Top SE

The first students graduated in 2006, and successfully achieved students are produced in every year. Following are what is the important changes mentioned by them.

  1. Improvement of modeling and analysis ability
  2. Improvement of understanding of systems and change in a viewpoint
  3. Extension of their knowledge and personal relationships by interacting with other students

Beyond the Top SE!

Top SE proposes collaborative study workshop with foreign university, step up to the doctorate course, and so on.

  1. Project-based study workshop with University College London in every year.
  2. Stepping up to the doctorate course and earn your doctorate.
  3. Graduated students can participate study meeting and exchange event.
